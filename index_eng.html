Eternal Faith

In the 23th Century, the highly developed technology had been completely indivisible with people's life, but the world's largest technology company, NJJC, thought that humans had become the most terrible thing on the earth, and in order to carry out race selection, NJJC tried to invent a virus, COVI-bola. However, out of his expection, the virus, which is highly contagious and had an extremely high mortality, would instantly turn the prosperous earth into a purgatory
However, it was found that the Wei Ze pass didn't suffer from the global disaster. Five surviving extraordinary young men formed the Sirius team and decided to go to that mysterious land to look for the antidote. In the Wei Ze pass, they found an ancient tomb detector, which may had the clue of the antidote. The murals of the tomb showed the glory of its owner's life, but only ended with a vague charactor "Zhao".
